Our story


Bloom Room has developed a unique visual language unparalleled in the floral industry. Our partnerships with various producers across the country enable us to offer a variety and quantity of flowers that cannot be found anywhere else in the Tampa Bay area. Our distinctive style has garnered local and national recognition. We constantly expand our creative range, and our seasonal blooming flowers always evoke a sense of being freshly picked from the garden. Thanks to our horticulturists and landscape architects, Bloom Room is like a magical garden, capable of transforming any space into a naturalistic fairytale with their keen sense of color and combination. We understand that flowers are not just gifts; they require special handling as they are sensitive to time and place. Our mission is to ensure unparalleled quality and style that touches the heart of each client and leaves a lasting impression.
