Learn about Roses

Roses encompass a wide variety of flowers, including many ancient types such as Galicia, Damask, Alba, and Centifolia (including the captivating Moss roses) that date back to before the mid-19th century.

'Charles de Mills' (1790) and 'Souvenir de la Malmaison' (1843) are examples of these old roses.

Learn about Carnations

Carnations are the national flowers of Monaco, Slovenia, and Spain. The red carnation is the state flower of Ohio.

Bogota, Colombia, is known as the world's leading producer of carnations, making it the capital of carnations
